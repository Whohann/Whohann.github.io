Starter Pokémon: Gallery

Starter Pokémon are the first companions you encounter on your journey in the Pokémon world. These special creatures are offered to you by a wise professor at the beginning of your adventure, and they serve as your introduction to the exciting world of Pokémon battles and exploration. You'll be presented with three choices: a Grass-type, a Fire-type, and a Water-type Pokémon. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, and your choice often depends on your personal preference or your understanding of type matchups. Your starter Pokémon will grow and evolve alongside you, becoming a powerful and loyal companion. They'll fight by your side in battles, explore new regions, and even help you overcome challenges. The choice is yours! Each starter Pokémon offers unique abilities and strengths, so choose the one that resonates with you the most and embark on your Pokémon journey together.
